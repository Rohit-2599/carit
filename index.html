Welcome to CARIT

The Center for Applied Research in Information Technology (CARIT) is dedicated to advancing technology through innovative research and industry collaboration. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic research and practical applications in the field of Information Technology.

We focus on cutting-edge areas including artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, cloud computing, and data analytics. Through partnerships with industry leaders and government agencies, CARIT provides unique opportunities for faculty and students to engage in meaningful research that addresses real-world challenges.

Our center serves as a hub for technology innovation, offering state-of-the-art facilities and fostering an environment where researchers, students, and industry professionals can collaborate on groundbreaking projects.
